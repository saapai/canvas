Not a notebook. Not a document.
A place.

Most tools organize your thinking into someone else's structure — folders, tags, timelines. Duttapad gives you a blank, infinite surface. Your thoughts live where you put them, exactly as you left them.

Double-click any entry to fall into it. Nest ideas inside ideas. Navigate your mind like a map.

Canvas

Infinite in every direction

Pan, zoom, place entries anywhere. No pages, no margins, no end.

Ink

Words that melt in

Each entry dissolves into the page with a hand-written ink-bleed animation. Thoughts feel physical.

Depth

Trenches

Double-click any entry to open a new canvas inside it. Nest infinitely — ideas within ideas within ideas.

Intelligence

Semantic organization

Press organize and an LLM reads your entries, understands them, and arranges them into a meaningful spatial layout.

Media

More than text

Paste a URL for a live preview. Search for movies, songs, or drop in images. Render LaTeX from plain English.

Public

Your page, your URL

Every canvas lives at duttapad.com/you. Share it, or keep it as your own private thinking space.
