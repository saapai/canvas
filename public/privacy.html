Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Duttapad ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website duttapad.com and related services. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Duttapad:

  • Phone number — used for account verification via SMS. We send a one-time verification code through Twilio to authenticate your identity.
  • Username — chosen by you to identify your canvas and serve as your public URL.
  • Canvas entries — the text, positions, media cards, link cards, LaTeX data, and other content you create on your canvas.
  • Uploaded images — images you upload to your canvas, stored via Supabase.
  • Google account data — if you connect Google Calendar, we store OAuth tokens to fetch your calendar events. We access calendar data in read-only mode.
  • Background preferences — your chosen canvas background settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account via SMS verification.
  • Store and display your canvas entries.
  • Provide features such as media cards, link previews, LaTeX rendering, and calendar integration.
  • Process text through AI models (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini) for semantic organization and LaTeX conversion.

3. SMS/Text Messaging

Duttapad uses Twilio to send SMS verification codes to your phone number during sign-in. By providing your phone number, you consent to receive these messages.

  • Program name: Duttapad
  • Purpose: One-time verification codes for account authentication.
  • Message frequency: Messages are sent only when you initiate a sign-in. Typically 1 message per sign-in attempt.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Contact your carrier for details.
  • Opt-out: You can stop receiving messages at any time by texting STOP to the number that sent you the verification code. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
  • Help: For assistance, text HELP to the number that sent you the verification code, or contact us at the email below.

We do not share your phone number or any information collected through SMS with third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number is used solely for authentication and account management.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by the following services solely to provide Duttapad's functionality:

  • Twilio — SMS verification delivery.
  • OpenAI — Text processing and LaTeX conversion.
  • Supabase — Image storage.
  • Google APIs — Calendar integration (only if you connect your Google account).
  • TMDB / Spotify — Media search functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, HTTP-only cookies, and secure authentication tokens. Deleted entries are soft-deleted (marked as deleted rather than permanently removed) to allow for recovery.

6. Your Public Canvas

Your canvas is publicly viewable at duttapad.com/your-username. Anyone with your URL can view your canvas entries. Only you (the authenticated owner) can create, edit, or delete entries.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through your canvas.
  • Delete individual entries from your canvas.
  • Change your username.
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (e.g., Google Calendar).
  • Request full deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8. Cookies

We use a single HTTP-only authentication cookie (auth_token) to maintain your login session. This cookie expires after 30 days. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Duttapad is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@duttapad.com.
